Rules

  1. Objective: Guess the secret five-letter word within six attempts.
  2. Guesses: You have six chances to guess the correct word.
  3. Feedback:
    • Correct letters in the correct position are highlighted green.
    • Correct letters in the wrong position are highlighted yellow.
    • Incorrect letters are not hightlighted.
  4. Word List: The secret word is chosen from a predetermined list.
  5. Word Length: The secret word is always five letters long.
  6. Letter Validity: Guesses must consist of valid five-letter English words.
  7. Winning: You win by correctly guessing the secret word within six attempts.
  8. Losing: If you don't guess the word within six attempts, you lose the game.
  9. Strategy: Use feedback from previous guesses to narrow down possibilities and make informed subsequent guesses.
  10. Replay: Players can play multiple rounds, trying to guess different secret words.
